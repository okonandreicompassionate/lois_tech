How we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our storefront, use our technical services, or interact with us digitally.

LoisTech values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our storefront, use our technical services, or interact with us digitally.

Last Updated: June 4, 2026

01

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information directly from you, including:

  • Contact Data: Name, phone number, email address, and physical/delivery address.
  • Business/Project Data: Specifications regarding your technical requests, hardware requirements, or billing details.
  • Device Information: Make, model, serial numbers, and technical state of hardware left in our care for repairs or upgrades.
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our technical services.
  • Process payments, issue invoices, and manage client accounts.
  • Communicate with you regarding project updates, service completions, or support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and maintain secure business operations.
03

Sharing Your Information

LoisTech does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may only share information with:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist us in operating our business (e.g., secure payment processors or delivery logistics).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, regulation, or court order to protect our rights or the safety of others.
04

Data Security

We implement practical administrative and technical security measures to protect your personal data and the hardware left in our custody. However, no electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

05

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections to inaccurate information, or request the deletion of your data once our service contract and legal obligations are fulfilled.

06

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

07

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact LoisTech directly at your designated business email or phone number.
