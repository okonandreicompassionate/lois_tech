What We Build

Four disciplines. One system.

Every LoisTech solution is planned at the blueprint stage and delivered under the same 4-phase SOP — so infrastructure, security, ecosystems, and furnishing arrive as one coherent build, not four separate vendors.

Backbone first

01 · Infrastructure & Connectivity

A backbone built for concrete, not drywall

Nigerian builds are dense — thick concrete, deep plots, multi-storey layouts. We design Wi-Fi 7 and structured cabling around that reality, not around a spec sheet written for a different climate.

  • Mesh access-point mapping tied to the architectural drawing, before walls close
  • Structured cabling for zero-lag streaming, gaming, and remote work
  • Redundant failover between fibre and LTE/5G backup links
  • Whole-property signal audit and heat-mapping post-install
Data stays home

02 · Privacy-First Security

Local control, not a cloud subscription

AI-driven CCTV and biometric access control that process and store footage on-site by default. You decide what leaves the building, and nothing does without that decision.

  • On-premise NVR storage with encrypted local backup
  • Biometric & keypad access control for gates, doors, and staff wings
  • AI-assisted motion and intruder detection with false-alarm filtering
  • Optional encrypted remote viewing — opt-in, never default
Hero Scenes

03 · Smart Ecosystems

Lighting, climate, and sound that read the room

One scene, one tap. Hero Scenes tie lighting, climate, audio, and shading together so a home shifts mood without anyone touching a wall panel.

  • Custom Hero Scenes: Arrival, Evening, Away, Movie Night, and more
  • Climate zoning per floor or wing, tied to occupancy sensors
  • Whole-home audio with per-room volume and source control
  • Voice, app, and wall-panel control — whichever the household prefers
Move-in ready

04 · Turnkey Interior Furnishing

A finished sanctuary, handed over complete

Our furnishing team procures and stages a property so the last step of a project is walking in, not waiting on a separate contractor.

  • Curated procurement aligned to the home's design language
  • Staging and installation coordinated around the technical handover
  • Furniture and fittings selected to complement automated lighting and climate
  • Final walkthrough and client training on every system, together
